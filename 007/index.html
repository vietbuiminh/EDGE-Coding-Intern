Thought for the Week

Time – such a precious commodity. We can never “get back” time. I used to think this meant time spent always had to be productive,
accomplishing something. Now I realize that time wasted is when I spent it not loving the people I was interacting with or
appreciating all the blessings I was currently enjoying – things like:

  1. My sight
  2. My mobility
  3. My hearing
What are you taking for granted today? Take some TIME to stop and love on whoever is in your presence this week and be grateful
for things you previously have taken for granted.
