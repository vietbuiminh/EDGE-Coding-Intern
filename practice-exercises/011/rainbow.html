Colors of the Rainbow

Welcome to my website describing the colors of the rainbow.

Everybody's heard of Roy G. Biv, right? In case you haven't, here's what it stands for:

Red

The color with the longest wavelength, from 622-780 nanometers. A nanometer is one to the ninth of a meter! Very small!

Orange

Oranges are nice and they taste great. Have you had any oranges today?

Yellow

The color with the smallest margin of wavelength, from 577-597 nanometers.

Green

The color that is observed the best by the human eye. Have you ever looked at a green laser?

Blue

A bit of a sad color. Doesn't blue make you sad?

Indigo

It is customary to list indigo as a color lying between blue and violet, but it has never seemed to me that indigo is worth the dignity of being considered a separate color. - Isaac Asimov

Violet

The color with the shortest wavelength, from 390-455 nanometers. Very fast!
